RestauranTour: Angeles City

Are you heading to Angeles City and want to know all the greatest places to eat? Or maybe you’re a local who wants to know where you can some good food on the regular? I got you!

It’s this week’s post of the RestauranTOUR series and 5 restaurants are being featured from Angeles City in the province of Pampanga -- the Culinary Capital of the Philippines!

Aling Lucing Sisig

Home of the original sisig! The namesake of this restaurant is actually the one who created the internationally known pork sisig back in the 1970s. They currently have 2 locations (in the Angeles City area) that are within walking distance of each other. They also have various methods of communication as seen below.

Branch 1 - Glaciano Valdez St., Brgy. Agapito del Rosario, Angeles City

Facebook: Aling Lucing Sisig
Telephone: 0906 288 8905
Delivery Partners: Fooddala

They are open from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM

From their original sisig to their other inihaw dishes, it truly is an iconic spot in Angeles City to try!

Mila’s Tokwa’t Baboy

Another restaurant that is famous for their sisig but of course, as their name suggests, their tokwa’t baboy as well! Their main branch is also known to have walls of pictures of its namesake (Mila) together with many different celebrities, local and international, who have eaten there over the years. Seen below are their current branches (in the Angeles City area) and contact details.

Branch 1 - San Andres St., Brgy. Sto. Domingo, Angeles City
Branch 2 - Hilda St., Brgy. Sto. Rosario, Angeles City

Facebook: Mila's Tokwa't Baboy
Telephone: 0975 362 9167
Delivery Partners: GrabFood, Mangan.ph, EatsPAPA

They are open from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM

They truly must have something worth trying since it’s a popular spot among celebs!

Angeles Fried Chicken

The place “where chicken tastiest heavenly!” as they say. Their specialty here is no doubt their delectable and juicy fried chicken. Their branches (in the Angeles City area) and ways to contact them are seen below.

Branch 1 - 1992 Marlim Avenue, Diamond Subdivision, Brgy. Balibago, Angeles City
Branch 2 - Upper Ground Floor, Newpoint Mall, Francisco St., Brgy. Sto. Rosario, Angeles City

Facebook: Angeles Fried Chicken
Telephone: (045) 322 4448
Delivery Partners: GrabFood, Foodpanda, Mangan.ph, Kooryr, LetsBee

They are open from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM

Did you know? Angeles Fried Chicken is the oldest running restaurant in Angeles City! How cool is that?

Paizano’s Pizzeria

Craving for some authentic New York-style pizza? You want cheese, pepperoni, or chicken bbq? Well, head to either of their 2 branches (in the Angeles City area) or contact them through the ways listed below.

Branch 1 - Zeppelin St., Hensonville, Brgy. Malabanias, Angeles City
Branch 2 - Sto. Rosario St., Brgy. San Jose, Angeles City

Facebook: Paizano's Pizzeria
Telephone: 0918 378 7133
WhatsApp: +63 915 963 2333
Delivery Partners: GrabFood

They are open from 11:00 AM to 9:00 PM (last order at 8:00 PM)

From their hand-tossed baked pizza to their New York-style sandwiches, you’ll certainly leave with your cravings satisfied!

Toll House

This place is the ultimate comfort food stop! It is a homegrown business so there is no doubt that there is love in each bite of their comfort food. Listed below are their 5 branches (in the Angeles City area) and the ways that you can contact them.

Branch 1 - Sto. Rosario St., Brgy. San Jose, Angeles City
Branch 2 - Ground Floor, Marquee Mall, Aniceto Gueco St., Brgy. Pulung Maragul, Angeles City
Branch 3 - Ground Floor, SM Clark, Manuel A Roxas Hwy., Brgy. Malabanias, Angeles City
Branch 4 - Second Floor, Newpoint Mall, Francisco St., Brgy. Sto. Rosario, Angeles City

Facebook: Toll House
Telephone: 0919 077 2210
Delivery Partners: Mangan.ph, PAPA.ph, Errandboy, LetsBee, GrabFood, Gogomove, Go2Guy Logistics, WeDrop.ph, EatsPAPA, Pabilis.ph

The times vary from branch to branch but typically they are open from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM

Some of their highly recommended specialties include baked macaroni, combination bbq, lengua, and many more! They also sell a variety of cakes.
